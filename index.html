What is Naada ?

Naada is rhythm, rhythm is sound, the sound is frequency, frequency is energy, energy is life, life is Naada. We are a bunch of music and tech enthusiasts who express and communicate our emotions through music and have found a way through technology to make it possible for the rest of humanity to do the same.
